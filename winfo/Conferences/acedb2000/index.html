Conference proceedings

The following is a combination of direct laptop-notetaking during conference talks or tutorials, June 12.-16., and material provided by task groups when needed. It should cover most of the conference proceedings, although no responsability is taken for errors in talks or missing material (perhaps due to bathroom breaks, eh?...)

Photographs from conference

Summaries of daily activities

Please follow links to more detailed reports for each day:

Monday 12.06.2000
First talks by the organizers and developers, settling in from a hardware perspective and task group formations

 
Tuesday 13.06.2000
An ACeDB Good/Bad/Ugly-discussion, improvements thoughts, task groups intentions and the dendrogram display

 
Wednesday 14.06.2000
Task group summaries, Ace-databases for the masses, multi- db connections, ideas on new sequence/genome comparative displays, XML discussion.
 
Thursday 15.06.2000
Socket client/server tutorial, CORBA/Java client/server, Distributed annotation system,
 
Friday 16.06.2000
Rather loosely structured really. Some of the participants left on Thursday, including Richard Durbin and Lincoln Stein (I think). So, it was a matter of doing some doc-writing or some more extra coding or whatever.

There wasn't really a formal end of the conference I think...I left myself for downtown Vancouver just after noon, to get to my new accommodations in time.
