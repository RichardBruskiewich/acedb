ACEDB User Group Newsletter - June 2004

If you want to have this newsletter mailed to you or you want to make comments/suggestions about the format/content then send an email to acedb@sanger.ac.uk.

This month has seen a lot of activity on a new ZMap project (a client version of FMap) by the acedb development team at Sanger and some writing activity on AceDB by Richard Durbin and Ed Griffiths, which adds up to a small newsletter for June.

General News

AceDB in print: a specialist review article on AceDB is to appear in "Encyclopedia of Genetics, Genomics, Proteomics and Bioinformatics" to be published soon by John Wiley & Sons. These articles are 1000 - 2000 words and offer the chance to give a overview of a particular area rather than a detailed technical description.

We'll let you know when its officially published.
