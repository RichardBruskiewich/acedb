OSP

OSP: Oligo Selection Program is a program by Ladeana Hillier and Phil Green to select oligos and score them in prevision of PCR experiments.

OSP and its documnetation are available from ftp.wustl.edu The program must be installed or recompiled and the osp executable should be in your path, possibly as /usr/local/bin/osp

The present program provides a graphci interface to OSP and a link to acedb.

Publications exist for OSP...see:

Hillier, L. and Green, P. (1991). "OSP: A computer program for choosing PCR and DNA Sequencing Primers," PCR Methods and Applications, 1, 124-128.

Gleeson, T. and Hillier, L. (1991). "A trace display and editing program for data from fluorescence based sequencing machines," Nucleic Acids Res., 23, 6481-6483.
